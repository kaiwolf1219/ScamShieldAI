🛡️ About ScamShield AI

ScamShield AI is a cybersecurity awareness and fraud-detection tool developed by Kai Ledbetter during the SANS Institute Find Evil Hackathon.

The project was created to help users quickly evaluate suspicious messages, emails, links, and contact information by analyzing common indicators of phishing, credential theft, and financial scams.

The application analyzes messages for common scam indicators such as:

  • Suspicious URLs
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Scam-related keywords

Built by Kai Ledbetter during the SANS Find Evil Hackathon.
