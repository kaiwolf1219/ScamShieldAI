About ScamShield AI

ScamShield AI was built by Kai Ledbetter as a project for the SANS Institute Find Evil Hackathon.

The project helps users identify suspicious messages, scam emails, phishing attempts, suspicious links, and possible fraud indicators.

Current Features

  • Keyword-based scam detection
  • Suspicious URL detection
  • Phone number detection
  • Email address detection
  • Risk scoring from 0–100
  • High, Medium, and Low risk classification
  • Possible scam category detection
